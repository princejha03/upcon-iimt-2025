Venue

IIMT College of Engineering, Greater Noida

About the Venue

IIMT College of Engineering is located in the heart of Greater Noida, providing modern facilities and infrastructure for academic conferences. The campus offers state-of-the-art auditoriums, conference halls, and accommodation facilities.

Address

Plot No. 19 & 20, Knowledge Park-III
Greater Noida, Uttar Pradesh, India - 201310

Transportation

Easily accessible by metro, bus, and taxi from Delhi NCR. The nearest metro station is Knowledge Park II on the Aqua Line.

Facilities

Modern conference halls with advanced AV systems, high-speed WiFi, air conditioning, and catering facilities.

Accommodation

Multiple hotels and guest houses available nearby. The organizing committee can assist with accommodation arrangements.

Venue Highlights

  • 500-seat main auditorium with advanced AV equipment
  • Multiple breakout rooms for parallel sessions
  • Exhibition area for poster sessions
  • Dedicated networking and catering spaces
  • Free WiFi throughout the campus
  • Ample parking facilities
